Welcome to the Real-time Functional Imaging and Neurofeedback community.

rtFIN is an international community dedicated to supporting neurofeedback research across all imaging modalities. We maintain a literature repository for all real-time functional imaging applications.

To receive updates from the rtFIN community, please subscribe to our email list. For information about the upcoming rtFIN conference in Nara, Japan, please visit the rtFIN 2017 home page. For further neurofeedback resources such as software and media, please visit our sister site, neurofeedback-research.org.
